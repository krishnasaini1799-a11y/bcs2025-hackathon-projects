About Shimla

Shimla, the capital of Himachal Pradesh, is known as the "Queen of Hills". Surrounded by snow-capped peaks and lush green valleys, it’s a perfect destination for nature lovers, honeymooners, and adventure seekers. With its colonial charm, bustling Mall Road, and panoramic views, Shimla attracts tourists from all over the world.

Top Places to Visit

Mall Road

Popular shopping street with restaurants, cafes, and shops.

Jakhoo Temple

Ancient Hanuman temple offering stunning views of Shimla.

Kufri

Famous for skiing and breathtaking snow-covered landscapes.
